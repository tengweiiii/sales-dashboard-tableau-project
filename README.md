# sales-dashboard-tableau-project
This Tableau project delivers two interactive dashboards to help stakeholders analyze business performance from both sales and customer perspectives. It enables sales managers, executives and marketing teams to monitor key metrics, identify trends and make data-driven decisions.

## Dashboards
### 1. Sales Dashboard
Provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, focusing on year-over-year comparisons and trend analysis.

**Key Features:**
- KPI summary: Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity (current vs previous year)
- Monthly trends for all KPIs with clear identification of peak and low periods
- Product subcategory comparison with sales and profit insights
- Weekly sales and profit trends with average benchmarks and performance highlights

### 2. Customer Dashboard
Offers insights into customer behavior, engagement, and profitability.

**Key Features:**
- KPI summary: Total Customers, Sales per Customer, and Number of Orders (current vs previous year)
- Monthly customer trends with highlighted high/low periods
- Customer distribution based on order frequency
- Top 10 customers by profit with detailed metrics (rank, orders, sales, profit, last order date)

## Interactivity & Filters
- Dynamic year selection for historical analysis
- Interactive charts for drill-down and filtering
- Navigation between dashboards
- Filters by product (category, subcategory) and location (region, state, city)
